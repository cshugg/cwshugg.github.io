experience

I'm a computer scientist with a passion for invention and security. I graduated from Virginia Tech in 2021 with a B.S. in computer science and a minor in cyber security. I completed a victory lap with a M.S., also in computer science, in the spring of 2022, focusing on computer systems and security, with an emphasis on fuzzing.

Research

During my M.S. degree at Virginia Tech (2020-2022), I created Gurthang, a fuzzing framework designed specifically for testing web servers. I introduced a novel design and showed its effectiveness through my evaluation. Read more about it here!

Career

In the past, I've had multiple internships with both Cisco and Microsoft. I'm super grateful for those opportunities and learned quite a lot at both companies. Starting in August of 2022, I'll be working as a software engineer at Microsoft.
I also worked as a Teaching Assistant with the Virginia Tech Computer Science department for five semesters. The majority of my time was spent as both an undergraduate TA and a graduate TA for CS 3214 - Computer Systems.
