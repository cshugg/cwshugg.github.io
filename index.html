get_creds

I'm a computer scientist with a passion for invention and security. I graduated from Virginia Tech in 2021 with a B.S. in computer science and a minor in cyber security. My victory lap will be complete in the spring off 2022 with a M.S., also in computer science, focusing on computer systems and security, with an emphasis on fuzzing.

Research

My M.S. thesis work has involved utilizing AFL/AFL++, an intelligent guided fuzzer, along with a series of libraries I have been creating, to perform effective fuzz-testing on web servers written in C. I've also put an education spin on things by integrating the fuzzer as an optional testing tool in Virginia Tech's CS 3214 course (Computer Systems). My research advisor is Dr. Godmar Back of the VT Computer Science department.

Career

In the past, I've had multiple internships with both Cisco and Microsoft. I'm super grateful for those opportunities and learned quite a lot at both companies. Starting in August of 2022, I'll be working as a software engineer at Microsoft.
