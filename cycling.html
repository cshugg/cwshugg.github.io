Cycling

While I've always liked riding my bike, I recently took an interest in road cycling. In December 2020, during my winter break, I was lucky enough to purchase an old 2004 LeMond Zurich racing bike. With lots of help from my dad, we fixed it up and made it a real speed machine.

I've made it my goal in 2021 to put a lot of miles onto this bike and get in shape.
